We believe legal documents should be as clear and minimal as our editor. Here is how we handle your data.

01. Data Ownership

You own everything you write. ViNotes is a local-first app; your notes stay in your browser's memory. If you don't sync to the cloud, we have no way to recover your data if lost.

02. Cloud Integration

When you use Google Drive Sync, we only access a private "App Data" folder. We cannot see, read, or modify any other files in your personal Drive account.

03. Zero Tracking

We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party cookies. Your productivity is private, and we intend to keep it that way. No data is ever sold.

04. Service "As Is"

ViNotes is provided without warranties. While we strive for perfection, we are not responsible for data loss due to browser crashes or account mismanagement.

Our Commitment

ViNotes is built on the principle of User Sovereignty. We provide the tools, but you keep the keys.

By using this application, you agree to take responsibility for your local storage and cloud sessions. We promise to never introduce features that compromise your privacy or clutter your creative process.
